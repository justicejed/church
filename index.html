Welcome to WIC

About Us

Word International Church (WIC) is a vibrant community dedicated to spreading the love and teachings of Christ. We strive to create a welcoming environment where individuals and families can grow spiritually, connect with others, and serve the wider community. Our mission is to empower believers to live out their faith in practical ways, making a positive impact in the world.

We offer a range of programs and activities, including weekly worship services, bible studies, youth groups, and outreach initiatives. Whether you're new to faith or a long-time follower of Christ, we invite you to join us on this journey of spiritual discovery and transformation.

Our Values

Biblical Truth
